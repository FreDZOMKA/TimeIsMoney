Time is Money Clock

How it works

Input your income per hour, day, week, month or year. The money clock takes this numbers and divides it by the chosen option. This number is then converted into seconds. When you start the clock every seconds corresponds to the amount of money made per seconds of time. For example, a month contains 720 hours or 2,592,000 seonds. The clock takes every seconds of everyday into account, not just working hours.
